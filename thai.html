Introduction

This page is dedicated to my study of the Thai language. I have spent about one year learning Thai. It is a fascinating language that is somewhat similar to how Hindi is structured in that there are 42 consonants and vowels as modifiers. I believe the technical terms are consonants which are just consonants and vowels are dipthongs.

The intent of this page is mainly to learn how to include foreign language typesetting within HTML. I also plan to learn JavaScript and make embedded applications and UI components such as quizzes and multiple choice activities.
